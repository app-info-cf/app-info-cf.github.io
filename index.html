Privacy Policy

About

IG Story Viewer app functions as a search engine for publicly available content. It is not affiliated with any social media platform and do not store any user content. The app may contain links to third-party websites, and IG Story Viewer has no control over the content or its availability on those websites. Please note that these links are provided for convenience and informational purposes only. The photos and videos featured in the search results belong to their respective owners.

Age restrictions

IG Story Viewer is intended for individuals aged 13 and above. We prioritize the safety and privacy of all users, especially minors. If you are under 13 years of age, please refrain from using our applications and ensure that you have the consent of a parent or legal guardian before accessing or utilizing any of our services.

Data collection

IG Story Viewer does not automatically collect users' personal data, except for information in cookie files saved by external services listed below.

Cloudflare Turnstile

IG Story Viewer utilizes Cloudflare Turnstile security to safeguard against a variety of internet bots attempting to exploit its resources. Cloudflare Turnstile gathers user-specific information to discern human interaction. For more information regarding the privacy aspects of Cloudflare Turnstile, please visit https://www.cloudflare.com/privacypolicy/.
